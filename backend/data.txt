Desire Infoweb - Company Knowledge Base

Company Overview
- Desire Infoweb Pvt Ltd is an IT offshoring and consulting company focused on Microsoft technologies.
- The company provides Microsoft 365 consulting and implementation services in multiple regions including Canada, USA, South Africa, and Australia.
- Core focus areas include SharePoint, Power Platform, Power BI, Office 365, Microsoft Teams, Dynamics 365, Azure, .NET, migration services, automation, and AI/chatbot solutions.
- Positioning statement highlights: backing teams and empowering businesses with tailored solutions, migrations, governance, and ongoing support.
